we harvest our products with the best quality and care Harvesting is the work of collecting the plant or part of the plant of interest for which the crop was sown, which can be: roots, stems, leaves, flowers, fruits and/or seeds. Each crop has its exact harvest season, which is defined by the product to be obtained. To achieve an adequate harvest, some aspects must be taken into account: harvest time, point of maturity (in the case of harvesting fruits or seeds), training of harvest personnel, type of harvest (manual or mechanized).
